Your Voice, Your Rights

When you record your voice on Kithly, that recording belongs to you. We believe your family stories, memories, and voices are sacred and should always remain under your control.

What this means:

You own all the audio content you create. Kithly simply helps you organize and share it with your family circle. We never claim ownership of your recordings.

Your family recordings are:

  • Owned entirely by you and your family members
  • Only accessible to people in your family circle
  • Never used for advertising, training, or any commercial purposes
  • Protected by privacy settings that you control

How We Handle Your Family Stories

Kithly's role is simple: we provide the technology to help families connect through voice. Here's exactly what we do with your content:

What we DO:

  • Store your recordings securely and privately
  • Create podcast episodes from your family's responses
  • Enable sharing within your family circle only
  • Backup your content to prevent loss

What we DON'T do:

  • Share your recordings with anyone outside your family
  • Use your content for advertising or marketing
  • Sell or license your family's voices
  • Train AI systems with your personal recordings
  • Monitor or listen to your private family conversations

Family Circle Guidelines

Kithly is designed for families to share authentic moments together. To keep our community safe and meaningful:

  • Family-only sharing: Only invite actual family members to your circles
  • Respectful communication: Keep conversations loving and appropriate for all ages
  • Privacy respect: Don't share other family members' recordings outside the app
  • Authentic sharing: Share genuine thoughts and experiences
  • Age-appropriate content: Keep all content suitable for family listening

Remember:

Every family member has the right to participate comfortably. If someone asks for their recording to be removed, please respect that request.

Your Account & Data

You have full control over your Kithly account and family data:

Account Control:

  • You can download all your family's recordings at any time
  • You can delete individual recordings or your entire account
  • You can leave family circles or remove family members
  • You control who can hear your recordings

Data Retention:

  • Your recordings are kept as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Individual recordings can be deleted immediately

Need to delete your account? Visit our account deletion page.
