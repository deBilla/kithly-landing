Privacy Policy

Your privacy and family's security are our top priorities

Last Updated: December 2024

Family-First Privacy Promise

At Kithly, we believe your family stories are sacred. We've built our entire platform around protecting your privacy and keeping your conversations secure within your family circle.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address for account creation
  • Profile information you choose to share
  • Family circle connections and relationships

Audio Recordings and Stories

  • Voice recordings you create in response to daily prompts
  • Audio content shared within your family circle
  • Transcriptions (processed locally on your device when possible)

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information (type, operating system, app version)
  • Technical logs for app performance and error resolution

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide the Kithly service: Deliver daily prompts, process recordings, and create family podcast episodes
  • Facilitate family connections: Enable sharing and listening within your private family circle
  • Improve the app: Enhance features based on usage patterns (using anonymized, aggregated data)
  • Provide support: Help you with technical issues and questions
  • Send important updates: Notify you about app updates, security issues, or policy changes

What We Never Do

  • Listen to or analyze your personal recordings
  • Share your stories outside your family circle
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your content for advertising or marketing
  • Train AI models on your personal conversations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Within Your Family Circle

Your recordings and stories are shared only with family members you've explicitly connected with in the app. Each family member controls their own sharing preferences.

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate Kithly:

  • Cloud Storage: Secure storage of your encrypted audio files
  • Audio Processing: Converting recordings to podcast format
  • Analytics: Anonymized app performance data

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and cannot access your personal content.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only when required by law, such as:

  • Valid legal subpoenas or court orders
  • Protecting the safety of our users or the public
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities

Data Security

We protect your family's stories with enterprise-grade security:

  • Encryption: All audio files are encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on who can access any data
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard cloud security practices

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all information we have about you
  • Download Your Stories: Export your family's recordings and transcriptions
  • Delete Content: Remove individual recordings or your entire account
  • Manage Sharing: Control what you share and with whom

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account:

  • All your recordings are permanently deleted
  • Your profile is removed from family circles
  • We retain minimal data only as required by law (typically 30 days for billing records)

Children's Privacy

Kithly is designed for families, including children under 13 with parental supervision:

  • Parents must create and manage accounts for children under 13
  • Children's recordings are only shared within the family circle
  • We don't collect personal information from children beyond what's necessary for the service
  • Parents can review and delete their children's content at any time

International Users

Kithly is available globally, and we comply with international privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional regulations. Your data may be processed in the United States, but always with the same level of protection regardless of location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We'll notify you through the app and email
  • We'll give you time to review changes before they take effect
  • We'll never reduce your privacy protections without your explicit consent
