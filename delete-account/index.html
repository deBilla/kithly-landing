What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following will be permanently removed:

  • All your audio recordings - Every story and response you've shared
  • Your profile information - Name, email, and account details
  • Family circle connections - You'll be removed from all family circles
  • Podcast episodes - Episodes containing your voice will be updated
  • App preferences - Settings, notifications, and customizations
  • Subscription data - Premium features and billing information

Impact on Your Family

üè† How This Affects Your Family Circle

Your deletion will impact other family members in the following ways:

  • Family members will be notified that you've left the family circle
  • Your voice will be removed from future podcast episodes
  • Past episodes containing your recordings will no longer be available
  • Family prompts will continue without your participation
  • Other members can continue sharing and connecting

Before You Delete: Download Your Data

We recommend downloading your personal data before deletion. This includes:

  • All your audio recordings
  • Transcripts of your responses
  • Family podcast episodes you participated in
  • Your account information and settings

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Through the App

1 Open Kithly and go to Settings
2 Tap "Account & Privacy"
3 Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
4 Follow the confirmation prompts

You'll need to verify your identity and confirm your decision.

Option 2: Contact Support

If you can't access the app or need assistance, contact our support team:

  • Email: support@kithly.app
  • Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion

Reactivation Policy

Once your account is deleted:

  • You can create a new account with the same email after 30 days
  • Your previous content and connections cannot be restored
  • You'll need to rebuild your family circles from scratch
  • Premium subscriptions are cancelled and cannot be transferred
