Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your family's stories are precious, and we believe your data is too. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and most importantly, how we protect it.

Kithly helps you create meaningful audio memories with your family. The process is simple:

  1. A user creates an account and gets a unique invitation code.
  2. They ask family members to download the app and join using that code.
  3. A prompt or question is created for the group.
  4. Family members answer the prompt using voice recordings.
  5. An "episode" is automatically created using everybody's recordings for the group to enjoy.

We only collect what's essential to make Kithly work:
• Account Information: Your email address and a nickname.
• Voice Recordings: The audio recordings you and your family submit to answer prompts.

We believe in data minimalism.
• Recordings are temporary: Individual voice recordings are permanently deleted right after an episode is created.
• Episodes are yours to control: Your completed episodes can also be permanently deleted by sending a request to dimuthu.billa@gmail.com.

To ensure your privacy, we want to be clear about what we don't do:
• No Transcriptions: We do not generate or store text transcriptions of your recordings.
• No Usage Tracking: We do not track or analyze how you use the app.
• No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share any of your personal data with third parties. Ever.
